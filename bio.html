Hi, I’m Travis Stahl

Realtor®, business professor, and founder of Trex Realty Group. With years of experience in both real estate and higher education, I’ve built my career on clear communication, trust, and a commitment to helping others succeed.

I teach business and real estate courses at Blinn College, and also help clients buy and sell homes across our community. My dual roles keep me sharp, informed, and passionate about serving people from all walks of life.

When I’m not working, I enjoy disc golf, woodworking, and spending time with my wife and son. I bring that same energy and attention to detail into every real estate transaction—because this business is about people first.

As a proud partner of Nobles Realty Group, LLC, I combine local expertise with modern marketing strategies to ensure your next move is the right one.
